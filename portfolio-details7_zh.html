The single "Sail Away" was the graduation song of Aletheia University in 2019. In this Song, I used the imagery of the Tamsui River and fishing boats sailing as the central theme, incorporating elements of jazz, Progressive rock, acapella. It serves as a record of my creative journey during my university years.
